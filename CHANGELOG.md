## v1.1 — 2026-06-29

Our second update — a big one. What's new for you:

- **Content Audit Studio** — paste any blog or article link (or your own draft) and instantly see how it scores for Google SEO and AI search, the exact issues holding it back, and how it compares to competitors — then rebuild a stronger version in one click.
- **One-click content creation** — turn a single topic or keyword into ready-to-publish blogs, ebooks, whitepapers, and LinkedIn posts, complete with visuals.
- **Smarter internal linking** — new content now links itself to your other relevant pages automatically (pulled from your sitemap), not just your homepage — better rankings and longer visits.
- **A beautiful new blog** — a fast, modern, mobile-friendly blog on your own domain, built to rank and pull in organic traffic, with a table of contents, FAQs, related posts, and search-engine structured data built in.
- **Publish in one click** — send finished posts straight to WordPress or Shopify.
- **Quality you can trust** — a built-in safety net means an incomplete or broken AI draft can never be saved, shown, exported, or published.
- **Never lose work, never pay twice** — audits and long tasks keep running even if you switch tabs or refresh, results come back on their own, and the same job is never charged twice.
- **Credit-smart audits** — audits now skip homepages and landing pages and handle redirecting links gracefully, so your research credits go only toward real articles.
- **A more polished experience** — refreshed light and dark themes, consistent navigation, smoother progress screens, and clean document downloads that look right in Word, Google Docs, and on Mac.
