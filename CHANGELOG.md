# Changelog

All notable Rankshoot product updates, newest first. Dates are YYYY-MM-DD.

## v1.4 — 2026-07-20

Our biggest update yet. The headline is **Content Audit Studio** — a new way to fix and level-up content you've *already* published — plus content that now generates in the background with real notifications, and a noticeable jump in how human and search-ready every article reads.

**Content Audit Studio (new)** — improve the pages you already have:

- **Audit any blog** by pasting a URL or your own content. You get clear scores for SEO, AI-search (GEO), answer-engines (AEO), overall quality, keyword demand, and freshness — plus the exact issues holding the page back.
- **Scan your whole site for free.** A fast, no-credit health check across all your pages that shows you which ones need attention first, so you can spend deep audits only where they matter.
- **One-click "Generate Enhanced."** Turns an audited page into an improved, ready-to-rank rewrite that fixes the flagged issues, keeps what already works, and covers what the top-ranking pages do — aimed at out-ranking them.
- **Schedule enhancements** straight onto your Content Calendar.
- **Download a shareable PDF** of any audit report.

**Content now generates in the background:**

- Start a blog and keep working — generation runs on our servers, so it finishes even if you refresh, close the tab, or drop offline. Queue several at once.
- After you hit generate you're taken to **Content History**, where each piece shows a live "Generating…" row and fills in on its own the moment it's done — no refresh needed.

**Notifications:**

- A new **notification center** (the bell) tracks everything running in the background.
- Optional **desktop/browser push** so you're pinged even on another tab or with the app closed.

**More human, more search-ready content:**

- Articles read more naturally and are far less likely to trip AI detectors — varied phrasing, no robotic clichés, and no stray em-dashes.
- Every article answers the reader up front, includes an FAQ, cites credible primary sources, and links to your own relevant pages.
- Cleaner output overall: correct titles, tidy meta descriptions, and no leftover formatting artifacts.

**Images:**

- Real, licensed images are pulled in automatically, with AI-generated images where needed — all delivered as fast-loading WebP.
- Cover images now publish to WordPress correctly.

**Keyword Discovery:**

- AI scores now appear **as they're calculated** instead of making you wait for the whole batch.
- Clearer keyword-difficulty labels and consistent columns across every table.
- Faster bulk scheduling and richer confirmation messages.

**Content Studio & editing:**

- Reworked "Ask AI" editing, in-place rewrites of selected text, and smoother full-screen editing.
- Choose from multiple AI topic ideas, and word-count settings that actually stick.

**Smoother all round:**

- Your in-progress forms are **saved automatically**, so you never lose work.
- The app keeps itself up to date in the background — no more hard refreshes.
- Faster page loads with loading states that match the final layout, so content no longer jumps around.

## v1.3 — 2026-07-09

Content Calendar improvements:

- **Schedule more than one thing per day** — put multiple keywords, blogs, ebooks, whitepapers, or LinkedIn posts on the same date instead of being blocked because it's "taken."
- **Pick your own date, even if it's busy** — the date picker shows which days already have content (dots under the date) and lets you schedule on one of them on purpose.
- **A cleaner calendar grid** — busy days show up to two content cards clearly, with a simple "+N more" for the rest instead of a cramped stack.
- **See content type at a glance** — every card now shows a small label (Blog, Ebook, Whitepaper, LinkedIn).
- **A tidier way to add content to a busy day** — hover a date and an "+ Add" button appears smoothly, right where you need it.
- **Faster and more responsive** — dragging a card to a new date moves it instantly, and confirmation messages now appear exactly when the change shows up on screen.
- Cleaned up repeated instructions on the Content Calendar page.

## v1.1 — 2026-06-29

Our second update — a big one. What's new for you:

- **Content Audit Studio** — paste any blog or article link (or your own draft) and instantly see how it scores for Google SEO and AI search, the exact issues holding it back, and how it compares to competitors — then rebuild a stronger version in one click.
- **One-click content creation** — turn a single topic or keyword into ready-to-publish blogs, ebooks, whitepapers, and LinkedIn posts, complete with visuals.
- **Smarter internal linking** — new content now links itself to your other relevant pages automatically (pulled from your sitemap), not just your homepage — better rankings and longer visits.
- **A beautiful new blog** — a fast, modern, mobile-friendly blog on your own domain, built to rank and pull in organic traffic, with a table of contents, FAQs, related posts, and search-engine structured data built in.
- **Publish in one click** — send finished posts straight to WordPress or Shopify.
- **Quality you can trust** — a built-in safety net means an incomplete or broken AI draft can never be saved, shown, exported, or published.
- **Never lose work, never pay twice** — audits and long tasks keep running even if you switch tabs or refresh, results come back on their own, and the same job is never charged twice.
- **Credit-smart audits** — audits now skip homepages and landing pages and handle redirecting links gracefully, so your research credits go only toward real articles.
- **A more polished experience** — refreshed light and dark themes, consistent navigation, smoother progress screens, and clean document downloads that look right in Word, Google Docs, and on Mac.
